Shopping in Goa

Shopping in goa is a unique experience
Administrator
12.01.2023

Goa is a beach lover's paradise, but shopping in Goa is an experience in itself. It is also a shopaholic's paradise and shopping in Goa is a real treat. What is special about this place when it comes to shopping?
Let's find out the markets that you should not miss while you are here.


1.) Anjuna Flea Market



Anjuna Flea Market is one of the most popular and amazing shopping places in Goa. Held every Wednesday at Anjuna Beach, this flea market is a true shopper's paradise. You will find hundreds of shops selling jewelry, clothes, wood crafts, shoes, handicrafts, hammocks, sheets, trinkets and carpets. There are many Indians and foreigners in this market. Show off your haggling skills at this shopping destination and pick up great items as souvenirs.
This market is at distance of 5 kms from AIDEN Vagator


2.) Panajim Market



For a true taste of traditional Goan shopping, visit Mapusa Market in Panaji on Friday mornings. You can buy spices, pickles, fruits, vegetables, fresh fish, cashew nuts, port wine, curry paste and more. But you can also search for cheap textiles, clothing, jewellery, and other items. Don't forget to test your bargaining skills at this great place and sample delicious food at nearby restaurants.
This market is at distance of 20 kms from AIDEN Vagator


3.) Mackie's Night Bazaar



A great place for keen shoppers, McKee's Night Bazaar takes place every Saturday afternoon until midnight at Baga Beach. There are over 100 shops selling souvenirs, handicrafts, clothing, jewelry, antiques and more. There are many restaurants and party venues, and you can enjoy shopping.
This market is at distance of 7 kms from AIDEN Vagator


4.) Aprora Night Market



The Arpora Market starts in the evening and continues until late at night. The market is bustling with local tourists and foreigners buying souvenirs. Here you'll find a variety of food stalls, fabric vendors, and knick-knack vendors selling a diverse selection of sundries. Around the market there are many clubs with ample parking spaces. You can go to these bars first for unlimited fun and then continue shopping.
This market is at distance of 6 kms from AIDEN Vagator


5.) Baga Market



The Tibetan Market is held on Baga Road from October to May, where you can find interesting jewelry and accessories for men and women. Apart from that, prayer flags, bells, centerpieces and even furniture are available at the market. Baga Beach has another popular market held every Saturday night. There you'll find numerous vendors selling jewellery, crafts, clothing, collectibles, jewellery, etc. With shacks and terrace cafés nearby, it's easy to get everyone's attention.
This market is at distance of 7 kms from AIDEN Vagator


6.) Calangute Market Square



One of the best places in Goa for shopaholics is Calangute Market Square, where you can find everything from summer clothes to jewellery. Here you can buy handicrafts, beachwear, leather goods, shells, carpets, jewelry and much more. Calangute Beach is lined with many huts and stalls, attracting all tourists in search of souvenirs.
This market is at distance of 10 kms from AIDEN Vagator


7.) Ashwem Beach Market



If you're looking for something unique when shopping in North Goa but still want some similarities, then you should head to Ashwem Beach's Wednesday Flea Market. There you can get everything that is currently in vogue. From sexy dresses to durable shoes to even bohemian handmade dresses, the market is available.
This market is at distance of 14 kms from AIDEN Vagator


8.) Mapusa Market



One of the best markets in Goa to experience a true taste of the city. If you are visiting Goa for the first time and have plans for a long vacation, you should go to Mapusa his market in your free time. Here you can buy unique textiles, antiques and souvenirs to take home. If you love traditional handicrafts, you'll want to explore the market at its best. There you will find Goan pottery, pickles, spices and more. Visit this place during the day as it closes at sunset. It's a long market, so I recommend taking your time exploring the market.
This market is at distance of 9 kms from AIDEN Vagator


9) CocoRoots



If you are a gem lover you must visit this place. This market has a huge collection of ethnic he jewelery made from coconuts, animal bones and horns (made from dead animals), so it's a great place to buy unique jewellery. You can present such incredible things and buy them as souvenirs that your friends and family will love.
This market is at distance of 3 kms from AIDEN Vagator


10) Ingo’s Saturday Night Bazaar



This is Goa's very famous market where you can see shopkeepers lining up many stalls with products such as carpets, fashion accessories, handicrafts, textiles and spices. You will be amazed to see amazing local Goan handicrafts that are not available anywhere else. Demonstrate your bargaining skills when purchasing products on the market. It is usually open from 16:00 to 23:00 from November.
This market is at distance of 6 kms from AIDEN Vagator
