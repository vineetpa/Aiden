Room overview

The Superior Rooms pay tribute to fashion and craftsmanship. With class-leading space and a choice between a Queen-sized bed or twin beds, you can enjoy the simple comforts of space with all the modern conveniences at hand in our Superior Rooms. All rooms are tastefully decorated and furnished and are en-suite with Bath and overhead showers. They are all fitted with basic amenities such as TV,double bed with quality material, Resting chair, Dressing mirror and 24 hrs running hot and cold water at the attached bathroom.
