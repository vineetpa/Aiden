Water Activities

Try water sports in North Goa for a fun vacation
Administrator
30.12.2022

Land of sun, sea and surf, Goa is like the blue-eyed boy that everyone loves. The scorching sun, quiet beaches, bohemian culture, authentic seafood and, of course, numerous party venues are enough to grab the attention of any visitor. There's a reason...exciting water sports! Yes you heard right. Water sports are not only the party capital of North Goa, but also give travelers an adventurous reason to explore the city. Safe and verified provisions allow you to try adventurous sports on almost any beach in North Goa. To bring out the adventure junkies in you, we've put together a list of the top water sports you should definitely try.

In Goa, it's common to enjoy the nightlife on the beach and explore the streets. Why not try some exciting water sports next time? Here is a short list of watersports you must try in North Goa.


1.) Kneeboarding



One of the most fascinating types of water sports, kneeboarding is the first step before trying more exciting sports. Combining surfing, waterskiing and wakeboarding, it's one of the easiest water sports to start. All you have to do is sit on your knees while balancing on the board and listen to your instructor.


2.) Windsurfing



Windsurfing is very racy and one of the most exciting activities in Goa. It's a hybrid of surfing and sailing, changing direction as the wind changes. As the most popular water sport, you can easily try it on any beach in North Goa.


3.) Jet Skiing



North Goa's most fun and popular water sport. Jet skiing is a great activity, especially for speed enthusiasts. Horseback riding in the Arabian Sea with the breeze on your face is a must try regardless of your comfort level. Continue to Candolim, Miramar and Baga Beach to experience the best of jet skiing.


4.) Kayaking



Tired of hard water sports? Choose a quiet and fun activity of kayaking. Easy to learn, it's the perfect water sport if you just want to paddle quietly through the mangroves and backwaters. Palodem Beach is one of the best places to try kayaking, followed by Donna Hepaura Beach and Holland Beach.


5.) Banana Tubeboat ride



Would you like to experience an exciting ride with your friends and family? Then a banana ride on her tube her boat is perfect. Take you and your group underwater on a giant banana-shaped boat for the most exhilarating experience. He is one of the easiest water sports that does not require swimming level.

North Goa's list of water sports includes snorkeling, scuba diving and water zorbing. To experience the best water sports, from October he heads to beach country through May.
