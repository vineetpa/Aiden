Try your gaming skills with budget-friendly casinos in Goa

Administrator
20.12.2022

Goa is one of the dream destinations for young people, couples, friends and families and a hub for party lovers. Goa's famous casinos are not only known in India, they are known all over the world. Good food, drinks, great music, and an energetic atmosphere are the reasons why people love to explore Goa's top casinos. Don't forget the variety of games such as Blackjack, Rummy, Stud Poker, Baccarat and Flash Roulette that these casinos offer. If you are an amateur in casino games and gambling, here is a list of budget-friendly casinos in Goa where you can put your gaming skills to the test.


1.) Casino Palms





If you're looking for a casino to visit with your family, Casino Palms is an ideal choice. With an entry fee of INR 1,000 per person, it offers exciting games, unlimited food and non-alcoholic beverages.


2.) Deltin Jaqk





Part of the Deltin Group, which owns the top casinos in Goa, Deltin Jaqk is one of Goa's budget-friendly casinos with around 350 different game positions. Jaqk offers casino guests a complimentary dinner buffet featuring great cuisine from all over the world. The INR 1,500 entry ticket also includes game chips worth INR 500.


3.) Casino Carnival





Held in two different venues, the Casino Carnival is known for its elegant interiors. Spanning four decks, this famous Goa casino has something for all ages. Various tickets are available from INR 1,500 per person on weekdays.


4.) Majestic Pride





With its spacious interior and flickering lights, the Majestic Pride sets the mood for an exciting night. Try your luck at a variety of Indian and international games with admission tickets from INR 2,000 (Bachelor Party). Various packages with different benefits are available.


5.) Chances Casino





Want to experience an old world casino? Then Chance Casino is for you. This casino located in Dona Paula offers a wide range of games with an entry fee of INR 750 in low season and INR 2,000 in high season.

6.) Crown Casino





A special decoration is a magnetic point that attracts guests. Huge transparent glass walls, a wide variety of gaming options, perfect views of the Mandvi River and the best gourmet selections - Crown He casino has it all. With an entry fee of INR 1,000, this casino is located at The Crown Goa, Panjim.


7.) Deltin Royale Casino



With a royal flair, this casino offers a gaming experience for both amateurs and professionals. The casino offers different packages ranging from INR 1,500 to INR 6,000. Each package includes food, drinks, live counter access and benefits depending on the package selected.
